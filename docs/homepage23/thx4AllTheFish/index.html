Origin slash First Sighting

Stories behind the frog tend to vary, but they usually go something like this: a salesman was driving along a road in Ohio in the fifties, when he saw three HUGE frogs in the road. The frogs then STOOD. UP. it's not really clear what happened next. some say they ran away, but other, way, way cooler interpretations involve the frogs disappearing with the use of magic wands.

Second and Third Sightings

In the seventies, a Loveland police officer was driving down the road when he came across a GIANT green thing in the road. He recounted it being about 4 feet long and having leathery skin. He then recalls it running to the side of the road, hunched over like a frog, before standing on two legs and climbing over the .. the thing that's on the side of the road. y'know. those. anyways, he told all his cop buddies and then a week or two later one of his friends saw what he believed was the exact same creature. so he shot it. as one does. And he took it back to the station where it was revealed to be....................................... ................................ an iguana. with no tail.

dot dot dot OR WAS IT. (aka Fourth Sighting)

In 2016, while playing pokemon go with his girlfriend, some guy saw the Loveland frog. He watched its eyes reflect in the light as it rose out of the water and walked around on its hind legs. it turned out that the frog, at least this time, was some high schooler named Jim. Jim said, and this is a direct quote, “I've been obsessed with the Loveland frog since I was a little boy. He's like my idol. I dress up as him and go to the tunnel and hop around a few times a week. I sometimes make little chocolates shaped as flies and I'll eat them while I hop around. And no, I don't think it's weird.” and that may be the weirdest thing i've ever read. no further sightings have been reported since then. at least, not until i go and catch one for myself.
