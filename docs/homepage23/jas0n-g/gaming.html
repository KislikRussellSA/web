Smash HDR

Smash HDR is a mod for Super Smash Bros. Ultimate. that aims to bring features from Super Smash Bros. Melee and Project M/+ as well as general gameplay balances. I manage to play it online using a Nintendo Switch emulator, Yuzu, but I'm not very good at it. If I had more free time, I would most likely practice it more because it is very fun.

I main the Ice Climbers! Their super difficult to master because you essentially have to control 2 separate characters at the same time. In vanilla Smash Ultimate, they can pull of some awesome combos!
